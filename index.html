About

Well hello there, I am a postdoc at the Institute for Computational Sciences at the University of Zurich in the group of Robert Feldmann, researching Deep Learning applications related to cosmological structure formation. My interests orbit around generative models, neural fields, galaxies and how and when the AI overlords will finally overthrow us.

Experience

Physicist by training and self-taught DL enthusiast. I hold a masters degree in Astrophysics and a PhD degree in the field of Data Science. I am proficient in Python, LaTeX, C++ and bash and my favourite environments for coding include PyTorch and TensorFlow.
