About

Hi, I am a postdoc at the Institute for Computational Sciences at the University of Zurich in the group of Robert Feldmann, researching Deep Learning applications related to cosmological structure formation. My interests orbit around generative models, neural fields, galaxies and the cosmic web.

Experience

Physicist by training and self-taught ML practicioner. I did a masters degree in Astrophysics and my PhD in the field of Data Science. I am proficient in Python and Cython, LaTeX, C++ and bash and my favourite environments for coding include PyTorch and TensorFlow.
